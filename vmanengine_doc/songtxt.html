Song.txt variables

These are the values for a song data's song.txt. Also used by other music, by having a txt file with the same name as the music.

1: BPM

The song's BPM, used in the menu.

2: Title

The song's title.

3: Characters

The song's characters separated by 2 colons (::). The first 3 are in order of player, opponent, girlfriend.

4: Composer

Whoever made the song.

5: Difficulties

List of the song's difficulties, separated by comma (,).

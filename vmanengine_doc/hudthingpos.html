Hud Thing Pos

These are the values for objects in HudThingPos variable in ui style's json file. These all have "Up" or "Down" put on the end (currently not optional), deciding whether or not they should be used in Upscroll or Downscroll. In order, the numbers are: X offset, Y offset, X fraction (of entire screen), Y fraction (of entire screen)

healthBar

Position of the health bar.

textHealth

Position of the text below the health bar.

textSide

Position of the text on the side of the screen.

textCorner

Position of the text in the bottom left corner of the screen.

textTimer

Position of the timer text.
